NYC for New Yorkers is a zine focused on the life we live every day as New yorkers. From trains to the streets our lives are tangled in a twisted web of akwardness, anger and a hint of empathy. We bring you the best stories from New Yorkers, the best places to visit and the things not to do in New York city for our non new yorkers readers.
